About Me

Hi, I'm GAURI TYAGI, a passionate web designer and developer and a love for creating intuitive, responsive websites and applications. With over few years of initial experience, I've had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects, from small business websites to large-scale web applications. My journey in web development began when I discovered my fascination with coding during Bachelor of Technology in CSE. I thrive on solving complex problems and transforming ideas into seamless digital experiences. I believe that a successful website is one that not only looks great but also provides a smooth & uninterrupted user experience. My approach to design is user-centric, ensuring that every element serves a purpose and enhances the overall functionality. I’m always excited to take new challenges and collaborate with.. Feel free to browse through my portfolio to see my work, and don't hesitate to get in touch if you’d like to discuss a project or just want to connect.
